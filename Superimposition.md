## Overview of pairwise structure superimposition tools:
- Sequential: 
  - [DALI - Pairwise](http://ekhidna2.biocenter.helsinki.fi/dali/)
  - CE
  - [TM-align](https://zhanglab.ccmb.med.umich.edu/TM-align/)
- Non sequential
  - GANGSTA+
  - MICAN
  - CLICK
- Flexible
  - Fatcat
  - Rapido
- Other: MetalS2
